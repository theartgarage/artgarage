ABOUT US

Welcome to Frenchmen Art Market, New Orleans' original weekly nighttime art market. We feature authentic art, dazzling jewelry, and creative crafts produced by local and regional artists. Listen to lively music while you browse, find gifts, and chat with local artisans. We're proud to offer a family-friendly cultural experience, so come experience the ambiance of the market combined with the flavor of Frenchmen Street.
