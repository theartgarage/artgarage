ABOUT US

The Art Garage, just steps away from the French Quarter, is a unique 500 person capacity venue with its own special charm thanks to our incredible lighting and art fixtures.

The Garage comes equipped with 4 restrooms, a PA system, generous amount of seating and the option for private VIP areas. In addition, we have a fully stocked bar with multiple bar options including an open or a cash bar.

We are a versatile space and are able to transform into whatever is needed. We have hosted a wide variety of events from weddings and receptions to shows and festivals.

Host your next party at the amazing Art Garage and you will be sure to bring your event to the next level!
